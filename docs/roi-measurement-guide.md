# ROI Measurement Guide

## Overview

AI sales automation should be measured with practical business metrics.

The goal is not to prove perfect attribution.

The goal is to understand whether AI-assisted workflows save time, improve consistency and support better sales execution.

---

## Metrics to track

### 1. Time saved

Track time saved on:

- Account research.
- Follow-up drafting.
- CRM note writing.
- Discovery preparation.
- Meeting summaries.

Example:

```txt
15 minutes saved per account research x 40 accounts per month = 10 hours saved per month
```

---

### 2. Output quality

Review quality improvements in:

- Account briefs.
- Follow-up emails.
- Discovery questions.
- CRM summaries.
- Objection responses.

Use a simple quality score from 1 to 5.

---

### 3. CRM hygiene

Track:

- Completeness of notes.
- Number of deals with next steps.
- Number of deals with clear pain points.
- Number of deals with identified stakeholders.
- Reduction in missing fields.

---

### 4. Sales activity support

Track:

- More accounts researched.
- Faster follow-up after meetings.
- More personalized outreach.
- Better prepared discovery calls.
- More consistent pipeline reviews.

---

### 5. Rep adoption

Track:

- Number of reps using the workflow.
- Number of workflows used weekly.
- Sales rep feedback.
- Manager feedback.
- Friction points.

---

## Simple ROI formula

```txt
Monthly time saved = minutes saved per task x number of tasks per month

Monthly value = monthly time saved in hours x estimated hourly value

ROI proxy = monthly value - monthly tool/process cost
```

---

## Example

```txt
Minutes saved per account research: 15
Accounts researched per month: 80
Total minutes saved: 1,200
Total hours saved: 20

Estimated hourly value: $50
Monthly value: $1,000

Estimated monthly process/tool cost: $200

ROI proxy: $800/month
```

---

## Important limitations

This is a practical ROI proxy, not a full financial model.

It does not prove that AI caused revenue growth.

For stronger measurement, combine time saved with:

- conversion rate;
- response rate;
- pipeline generated;
- deal velocity;
- win rate;
- sales cycle length;
- manager review.
