# Example AI-Assisted Account Brief

## Target account

Example Company

## Industry

B2B services

## Website

```txt
https://example.com
```

## Target buyer

Head of Sales

---

## Company summary

Example Company provides professional services to mid-market clients.

The company appears to rely on inbound leads, referrals and direct sales conversations.

---

## Possible business priorities

- Improve lead response time.
- Increase sales team productivity.
- Standardize follow-up quality.
- Improve CRM data quality.
- Reduce time spent on manual account research.
- Create more consistent discovery preparation.

---

## Possible pain points

- Sales reps spend too much time researching accounts manually.
- Follow-up emails are inconsistent.
- CRM notes are incomplete or difficult to use.
- Managers lack visibility into deal quality.
- Lead qualification is not standardized.

---

## Suggested outreach angle

Focus on helping the sales team save time and improve consistency with AI-assisted account research, follow-up drafting and CRM note structuring.

---

## Discovery questions

1. How does your team currently prepare for new account conversations?
2. How long does account research usually take?
3. What information do reps need before discovery calls?
4. How do you ensure CRM notes are complete and useful?
5. Where do reps lose the most time in the sales process?
6. Which parts of follow-up are currently repetitive?
7. Do managers have enough visibility into deal quality?

---

## Potential objections

### "We already use ChatGPT."

Possible response:

Using ChatGPT manually can help, but the main value comes from standardizing the workflow, inputs, outputs and review process across the team.

### "We do not want generic AI emails."

Possible response:

The goal is not to automate generic outreach. The goal is to create better first drafts and structured account insights that humans review and personalize.

### "Our CRM data is messy."

Possible response:

That is often the right starting point. AI-assisted workflows work best when paired with CRM hygiene improvements and clear field structures.

---

## Suggested next step

Run a small pilot with 10 target accounts.

Measure:

- Research time saved.
- Email quality.
- CRM note completeness.
- Discovery preparation quality.
- Sales rep feedback.
