# How to Use This Playbook

## Overview

This playbook provides practical AI workflows for B2B sales teams.

It is designed to help with:

- Account research.
- Lead qualification.
- Discovery call preparation.
- Follow-up email drafting.
- CRM updates.
- Objection handling.
- Sales productivity.

The goal is not to replace salespeople.

The goal is to reduce repetitive work, improve consistency and help sales teams spend more time on high-value conversations.

---

## Who this is for

This playbook is useful for:

- Account executives.
- SDRs and BDRs.
- Sales managers.
- Founders doing sales.
- Consultants.
- B2B service providers.
- SMB teams building sales processes.

---

## Recommended workflow

Use the playbook in this order:

1. Start with account research.
2. Use the lead qualification workflow.
3. Prepare discovery questions.
4. Draft the first outreach or follow-up.
5. Review and personalize manually.
6. Update the CRM with structured notes.
7. Track time saved and quality improvements.

---

## How to use the prompts

For best results:

- Provide clear input.
- Include company name and website.
- Include target buyer or persona.
- Include industry.
- Include known context.
- Ask for structured output.
- Review all AI-generated content.
- Personalize before sending externally.

---

## What AI can help with

AI can support:

- Summarizing company information.
- Identifying possible pain points.
- Drafting discovery questions.
- Creating account briefs.
- Structuring follow-up notes.
- Rewriting emails.
- Extracting CRM fields from notes.
- Generating objection handling ideas.

---

## What AI should not do alone

AI should not independently:

- Send emails without approval.
- Update CRM records without review.
- Make claims about prospects without verification.
- Invent customer pain points.
- Guess confidential information.
- Replace actual discovery.
- Make pricing or legal commitments.

---

## Human review rule

Every AI-generated output should be reviewed before use.

Review for:

- Accuracy.
- Tone.
- Relevance.
- Personalization.
- Confidentiality.
- Compliance.
- Brand alignment.

AI should support sales judgment, not replace it.
