# Workflow: AI-assisted Follow-up Email

## Objective

Create clear, personalized follow-up emails after calls, demos or discovery conversations.

## Input

- Prospect name
- Company
- Conversation summary
- Pain points
- Promised next step
- Timeline
- Relevant offer or resource

## Process

1. Extract confirmed discussion points and commitments.
2. Draft a concise follow-up with a clear next step.
3. Check that the message does not add unsupported claims.
4. Send the draft to the sales owner for editing and approval.

## AI output

- Draft follow-up email
- Subject line options
- Call-to-action
- Optional shorter version

## Business value

This workflow reduces drafting time and helps sales teams follow up more consistently after conversations.

## Human review step

- Verify all claims before sending.
- Remove exaggerated promises.
- Keep the tone natural and specific.
- Do not include confidential notes.

## Risks and limitations

- AI may misrepresent commitments or timelines.
- Generic wording can reduce personalization.
- Pricing, legal and contractual statements require explicit approval.
- The workflow should not send messages automatically without review.

## Suggested prompt

Act as a B2B sales assistant.

Write a concise follow-up email based on this conversation.

Conversation notes:
[notes]

Goal of the follow-up:
[goal]

Return:
1. Subject line
2. Email body
3. Shorter version
4. Suggested next step

Do not invent facts or commitments. If information is missing, mark it as unknown or ask for clarification. Keep the output concise and reviewable by a human sales representative.
