# Workflow: AI-assisted Account Research

## Objective

Help sales teams prepare faster before contacting a target account.

## Input

- Company name
- Website
- LinkedIn page
- Industry
- Target buyer
- Recent news or trigger event

## AI output

- Company summary
- Business priorities
- Possible pain points
- Buying triggers
- Suggested outreach angle
- Discovery questions
- Potential objections

## Business value

This workflow helps reduce research time, improve personalization and increase the quality of outbound conversations.

## Suggested prompt

Act as a B2B sales research assistant.

Analyze the following company and prepare a concise account brief for a sales representative.

Company:
[company name]

Website:
[website]

Industry:
[industry]

Target buyer:
[target buyer]

Return:
1. Company summary
2. Likely business priorities
3. Potential pain points
4. Suggested outreach angle
5. Discovery questions
6. Risks or objections
