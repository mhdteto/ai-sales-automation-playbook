# Workflow: AI-assisted Lead Qualification

## Objective

Help sales teams qualify inbound leads faster and route them to the right next action.

## Input

- Lead name
- Company
- Website
- Job title
- Industry
- Message or form submission
- Budget range if available
- Timeline if available

## AI output

- Company summary
- Lead fit score
- Main need or intent
- Suggested priority
- Missing information
- Recommended next step

## Business value

This workflow helps teams respond faster, prioritize stronger opportunities and avoid treating every inbound lead the same way.

## Suggested prompt

Act as a B2B sales qualification assistant.

Analyze the following inbound lead and prepare a concise qualification summary.

Lead:
[lead details]

Company:
[company details]

Return:
1. Company summary
2. Estimated fit: high, medium or low
3. Buyer intent
4. Missing information
5. Recommended next step
6. Suggested follow-up question
