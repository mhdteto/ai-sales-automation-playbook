# AI Sales Automation Playbook

Practical AI automation playbook for B2B sales teams: lead qualification, follow-up, CRM hygiene and account research.

## Why it matters

Sales teams lose time on repetitive research, manual CRM updates and inconsistent follow-up. AI can reduce that friction when it is used with clear workflows, structured prompts and human review.

This playbook focuses on practical use cases that improve sales productivity without replacing sales judgment.

## Use case

Use this playbook when a B2B sales team wants to introduce AI into daily workflows in a controlled, useful and business-focused way.

## Example

A sales representative can use the account research workflow before calling a prospect. The AI assistant returns a concise account brief, possible pain points, outreach angle and discovery questions.

## How to use

1. Choose the workflow that matches the sales task.
2. Copy the suggested prompt.
3. Replace the placeholders with real business context.
4. Review the AI output before using it externally.
5. Track time saved and quality improvements.

## Business value

The workflows help reduce research time, improve personalization, increase CRM quality and create more consistent sales follow-up.

## Disclaimer

This project does not guarantee pipeline generation, revenue growth, closed deals or sales performance improvement.

It provides practical templates and workflows to support sales execution, research quality and productivity.

AI-generated sales output should always be reviewed by a human before use.
