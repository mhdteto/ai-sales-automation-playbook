### Purpose

This first version provides a practical playbook for using AI in B2B sales workflows.

It is designed for:

- Sales teams.
- Account executives.
- SDR / BDR teams.
- Sales managers.
- Consultants.
- B2B service providers.
- SMBs building repeatable sales processes.

### Notes

This project does not guarantee pipeline generation, revenue growth, closed deals or sales performance improvement.

It provides practical templates and workflows to support sales execution, research and productivity.

AI output should always be reviewed by a human before being sent to prospects or added to a CRM.
