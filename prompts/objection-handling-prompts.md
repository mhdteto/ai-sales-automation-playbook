# Objection Handling Prompts

## Price objection

Act as a sales coach.

The prospect said:
"[objection]"

Context:
[deal context]

Return:
1. What the objection likely means
2. Clarifying question
3. Value-based response
4. Risk to avoid

## Timing objection

Act as a B2B sales coach.

The prospect says now is not the right time.

Context:
[context]

Return:
1. Possible reason behind the objection
2. Follow-up question
3. Useful response
4. Suggested next step

## Internal approval objection

Act as a sales assistant.

The prospect needs internal approval.

Return:
1. Questions to clarify the approval process
2. Information to provide
3. Follow-up email angle
4. Risk signals
