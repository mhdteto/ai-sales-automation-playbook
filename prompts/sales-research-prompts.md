# Sales Research Prompts

## Company overview

Act as a B2B sales research assistant. Summarize this company in 5 bullet points for a sales representative.

Company:
[company name]

Website:
[website]

Constraints:
- Use only provided or verifiable public information.
- Do not invent facts, priorities or trigger events.
- Mark missing information as unknown or assumption.
- Keep the output concise and structured.
- A human sales representative must review the output before use.

[event]

[company]

Constraints:
