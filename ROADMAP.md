# Roadmap

## Current version

v1.0 - Initial public version of the AI Sales Automation Playbook.

## Purpose

This project provides practical AI workflows, prompts and templates for B2B sales teams.

The goal is to help sales teams save time, improve research quality, qualify leads more consistently and standardize repetitive sales workflows.

## Current scope

The current version includes:

- Account research workflow.
- Lead qualification workflow.
- Follow-up email workflow.
- CRM update workflow.
- Sales research prompts.
- Discovery call prompts.
- Objection handling prompts.
- Basic ROI calculator.
- Documentation and usage guidance.
